About Company

Barkas Teks LLC is dynamically developing company in Uzbekistan, specializing in production of wide range of terry fabrics and terry products: towels and bathrobes. Currently, the quality and competitiveness of the production of terry products depends on the equipment on which these products are produced. In order to ensure production of high quality, competitive terry products our company on an ongoing basis implements modern high-tech equipment of the leading manufacturers of textile machinery.

More

Barkas Teks LLC is dynamically developing company in Uzbekistan, specializing in production of wide range of terry fabrics and terry products: towels and bathrobes. Our company is located at beautiful landscapes of Kasansay District in Namangan Region. Starting from August 1999, the date of its establishment, Barkas Teks LLC has been gradually increasing its terry products production capacities. Presently production capacity of our facilities is 2400 tons per annum. Despite the short period of functioning, our company terry products gained customer trust and preferences not only in Uzbekistan, but also abroad. We are successfully exporting our products to Russia, Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. We bring comfort and coziness to your home! Barkas Teks LLC is always at your service!!!
