About Company

BARKAS-TEKS GROUP is a rapidly developing company in the Republic of Uzbekistan specializing in the production of terry fabrics and terry products: towels and bathrobes.
Modern and world-class technology allow us to produce high-quality products that provide high competitiveness in both domestic and foreign markets.
Besides, it meets the requirements of both international standards the requirements of the consumers.
All the products we offer are made on the basis of environmentally friendly raw materials and they absolutely do not cause any allergic reactions and are completely safe for human health.
Our production capacity allows us to produce from 18 - 20 tons of terry products per day.
New quality control systems are being introduced, constant work is being carried out to find the most high-quality raw materials for the production of our products.
Today, We are one of the leading suppliers of terry products in Uzbekistan..

More

Barkas-teks Group is dynamically developing a group of companies in Uzbekistan, specializing in production of wide range of terry fabrics and terry products: towels and bathrobes. Our company is located at beautiful landscapes of Kasansay District in Namangan Region. Starting from August 1999, the date of its establishment, Barkas-teks Group has been gradually increasing its terry products production capacities. Presently production capacity of our facilities is 7200 tons per annum. Despite the short period of functioning, our company terry products gained customer trust and preferences not only in Uzbekistan, but also abroad. We are successfully exporting our products to Russia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Ukraine, Belarus, Moldavia and Tajikistan. We bring comfort and coziness to your home!
