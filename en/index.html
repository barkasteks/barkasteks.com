About Company

BARKAS-TEKS GROUP is one of the top rapidly developing companies in the Republic of Uzbekistan specializing in the production of terry products: towels and terry fabrics.
The latest advanced technology allows us to produce high-quality products that provide high competitiveness in both domestic and foreign markets, it meets the requirements of international standards and the requirements of the consumers.
All the products we offer are made on the basis of environmentally friendly raw materials, so that it does not cause allergic reactions and is completely safe for human health.
The production capacity allows us to produce from 18 - 20 tons of terry products per day. New quality control systems are being introduced, constant work is being carried out to find the highest quality raw materials for the production of our products.
Today, We are one of the leading suppliers of terry products in Uzbekistan.
