About Company

Barkas Teks LLC is dynamically developing company in Uzbekistan, specializing in production of wide range of terry fabrics and terry products: towels and bathrobes. Currently, the quality and competitiveness of the production of terry products depends on the equipment on which these products are produced. In order to ensure production of high quality, competitive terry products our company on an ongoing basis implements modern high-tech equipment of the leading manufacturers of textile machinery.
